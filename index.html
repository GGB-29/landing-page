This is Maomao.
Maomao is a clever, sharp-tongued apothecary with a knack for poisons and deduction, who unintentionally attracts palace intrigue despite her best efforts to stay unnoticed.
Related Characters
Jinshi is a high-ranking, mysterious palace official who gradually forms a deep, protective, and romantic bond with Maomao.
Xiaolan is a clever and observant courtesan-in-training with a sharp tongue and keen mind, navigating palace intrigues with wit.
Shisui is a graceful and mysterious imperial consort with a hidden past and a subtle, complex influence within the inner palace.
Lakan is a powerful and eccentric nobleman, who harbors a mysterious connection to Maomao.
